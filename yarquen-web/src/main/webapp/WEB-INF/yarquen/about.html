Yarquen is a mapuche word that means "owl". Owls characterize themselves for been highly capable in the detection and capture of their prey, even in dark environments. The author of this crawler thought that those characteristics are desirable for a focused crawler ;)

Yarquen is a focused, semantic, distributed crawler. Yarquen is specialized in the recollection, classification and analysis of technological related content, like articles, presentations or blog post of sites like Infoq and Dzone.

Additionally, and in order to exploit and present the data collected by the crawler, the ContentCompass team built a web application (this one!) that provides faceted search, social enrichment and other nice features.

History

Yarquen began as an experimental, open source, alternative to the official ContentCompass crawler, mainly to "play" with big data and certain technologies.
